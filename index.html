Aboutme

My name is Letícia Queiroz, and my professional journey has been, shall we say, a little unusual.

I started out in a completely different field: I graduated in Veterinary Medicine and spent years working in hospitals and research. That was a phase of deep commitment, where my mission was to improve lives through precision and care.

But, you see, technology has always been my secret passion. Before university, I took a technical course in Game Programming, and I even did some web development work early on. Even with veterinary medicine taking center stage, I never let that tech side die out.

Recently, I made a decision to reconnect, it was time to return to where my heart always belonged. I’m currently in a full transition to software development. I’ve been diving deep into Front-End (HTML, CSS, JavaScript) and discovering that Back-End is the area that truly fascinates me and where I want to build my career.

I see technology as a powerful tool to transform lives and solve real problems, and that is the exact same drive I had in veterinary medicine. Ultimately, I believe both fields share the same core essence: improving the world through dedication, precision, and, above all, empathy.
